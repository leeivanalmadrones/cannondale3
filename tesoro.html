Tesoro

At home in the city, yet always ready to break free, the Tesoro is your ticket to the great outdoors. Like an Interrail pass on two-wheels, this adventurous commuter brings wild vistas within reach and makes distant peaks just a ride away. With huge range, robust components, and grippy mixed-surface tires, the Tesoro is built to get you to it all week, and away from it all on the weekend.
